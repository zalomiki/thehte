How to Learn Best

Simple tips to make your learning more effective

Repeat After the Video

When you hear new words, say them out loud. Try to copy how the person speaks. Do this 2-3 times for each video.

Save Favorite Videos

Make a collection of videos you like. Watch them again later to remember better. Try to watch your favorites 3 times over a week.

Use the Words Soon

After learning new words, try to use them the same day. Say them to yourself, write them in a message, or tell a friend.
